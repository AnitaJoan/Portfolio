Gamification of the British Museum Experience

CONCEPT - 2 WEEK SPRINT - 4 PERSON TEAM

Brief

We were asked to create a highly engaging experience that helped visitors to the museum learn more about artefacts they liked and interact with the exhibits. Visitors must be able to track what they have enjoyed and share those discoveries on social media.

Process & Deliverables

    Discover
  • Site Visit
  • Competitive Analysis
  • User Interviews x13
  • Data Synthesis
    Define
  • Persona
  • Experience Map
  • 'How might we questions'
    Develop
  • Design Studio
  • Sketches
  • Mid-fi Wireframes
  • User Testing x5
    Deliver
  • Hi-Fi Mockup
  • Clickable Prototype
  • 20min Presentation
  • Spec Document

Solution

We developed a game that connected museum artefacts with a storyline. This turned the museum experience into an educational scavenger hunt. We adapted the key features of a game to the traditional learning and viewing experience which was very well-received by users.

Overview of Key Deliverables

Site Visit

To understand how people interact with the exhibits currently.

Top 3 insights
  • Most wander around
  • Desire to read dwindles over time
  • Take photos of things they like
Data Synthesis

To consolidate raw data and identify trends.

Top 3 insights
  • Value story & interaction
  • Prefer a linear journey
  • Too much to read
Design Studio

To rapidly generate design ideas.

Top 3 ideas
  • Fun facts scavenger hunt
  • Photo bingo
  • 3D interactive artefact view
Prototype

To test redesigns with users and present the concept.

Software used
  • Marvel for paper prototypes
  • Sketch for mid and hi-fi designs
  • InVision for final prototype
