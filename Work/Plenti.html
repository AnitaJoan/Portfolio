Plenti Household Stock Manager App

CONCEPT - 2 WEEK SPRINT - SOLO

Brief

Our first project at General Assembly was an open brief: discover a problem your friend has and develop an app to solve it. Through user interviews I uncovered Holly's frustration when she runs out of Nespresso Coffee Pods. I challenged myself to not only solve this problem but develop a solution that would be widely applicable.

Process & Deliverables

    Discover
  • User Interviews x2
  • Data Synthesis
    Define
  • Storyboard
  • Problem Statement
  • Experience Map
    Develop
  • Sketches
  • User Flow
  • Mid-fi Wireframes
  • User Testing x7
    Deliver
  • Hi-Fi Mockup
  • Clickable Prototype
  • 5min Presentation

Solution

I designed an app to calculate the use of household essentials and set reminders to buy them. From this project, I learnt the basics of the UX process and how important both research and testing is to dissolving any assumptions I might have about my users.

Overview of Key Deliverables

User Interviews

To uncover pain points in Holly's routine.

Top 3 insights
  • Loves her Nespresso machine
  • Often forgets to buy coffee pods
  • Has to wait 4 days for delivery
Storyboard

To visualise Holly's frustration and define the experience map.

Top insight

Solve the first low point and the rest of the experience doesn't occur.

User Testing

To assess the viability and usability of the prototype.

Top 3 insights
  • Easy and straightforward to use
  • Too many screens and questions
  • Applicable to all things people forget
Prototype

To test redesigns with users and present the concept.

Software used
  • Marvel for paper prototypes
  • Sketch for mid and hi-fi designs
  • Sketch for final prototype
