Plenti Household Stock Manager App

CONCEPT - 2 WEEK SPRINT - SOLO

Brief

Our first project at General Assembly was an open brief to familiarise us with the UX process with more focus on definition and development.

Through interviews, I discovered that my friend Holly gets very frustrated when she runs out of Nespresso Coffee pods.

Process & Deliverables

    Discover
  • User Interviews x2
  • Data Synthesis
    Define
  • Storyboard
  • Problem Statement
  • Experience Map
    Develop
  • Sketches
  • User Flow
  • Mid-fi Wireframes
  • User Testing x7
    Deliver
  • Hi-Fi Mockup
  • Clickable Prototype
  • 5min Presentation

Solution

Through a storyboard and experience map I identified the target pain point to focus on: the moment Holly forgets to buy more coffee pods.

I designed an app to calculate the use of Hollyâ€™s household essentials and automatically set reminders to buy them when stock was low.

Overview of Key Deliverables

User Interviews

To uncover pain points in Holly's routine.

Top 3 insights
  • Loves her Nespresso machine
  • Often forgets to buy coffee pods
  • Has to wait 4 days for delivery
Storyboard

To visualise Holly's frustration and define the experience map.

Top insight

Solve the first low point and the rest of the experience doesn't occur.

User Testing

To assess the viability and usability of the prototype.

Top 3 insights
  • Easy and straightforward to use
  • Too many screens and questions
  • Applicable to all things people forget
Prototype

To test redesigns with users and present the concept.

Software used
  • Marvel for paper prototypes
  • Sketch for mid and hi-fi designs
  • Sketch for final prototype
