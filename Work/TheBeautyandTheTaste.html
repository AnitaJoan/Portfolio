Redesigning The Beauty and The Taste Wine Website

CLIENT - 2.5 WEEK SPRINT - 4 PERSON TEAM

Brief

The Beauty and the Taste are a brand new online retail boutique, specialising in handpicked, high-rated fine wines from all over the world. They believe that good stories drive meaningful purchases. Our job was primarily to reduce the bounce rate and identify any navigation blockers.

Solution

We redesigned problem pages to focus more on sales and removed the mysticism from the information architecture and content hierarchy. We added a special Taste Profile feature that created personalised search results which was very well recieved by users.

Overview of Key Deliverables

User Tests & Interviews

To understand first impressions, ease of navigation and influences of purchase.

Top 3 insights
  • Storytelling concept was unclear
  • Looking for guidance
  • Too much to read
Experience Map

To locate problem pages and identify opportunites.

Top 3 insights
  • Homepage doesn't meet expectation
  • Product page lacks key facts
  • Checkout is smooth
Persona

To narrow focus to one type of user.

Pain points
  • Wants quick, scannable information
  • Confused by wine terminology
  • Needs assurance and guidance
Prototype

To test redesigns with users and present ideas to the client.

Software used
  • Marvel for paper prototypes
  • Sketch for mid and hi-fi designs
  • InVision for final prototype
