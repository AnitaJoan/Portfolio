PROCESS & OUTCOME

By interviewing entrepreneurs and intermediaries with previous experience with our client, we were able to capture and amplify their strengths and better match their brand strategy to the truth of their service: a highly specialist, human touch experience. This resulted in a brand refresh and new website designed for user needs.

CHALLENGES

Because we have to compete with so many old and established competitors, finding what made them the same and our client special was challenging. The breaktrhough came when we started to see patterns in how previous clients described the mismatch between their current website and their expectations of our client and their actuall human experience of them.

As with a lot of our projects, this had a high learning curve as I had no understanding of Private Equity vs Venture Captialists. However much of this project ended up being about how to communicate with your fellow man.


OUTCOME

Our client was quite skeptical of our methods in the beginning but we persisted and were able to show them an extensive research canvas, identifying white space in colour, typography, logo devices and visual language as well as what their clients would be suprised by.

My role in the project

INSIGHTS ANALYSIS

I partnered with a UX Researcher to translate interview insights into analyses frameworks and insight summary canvas which we used to quickly communicate the meat of our findings to the client.

FEATURE ANALYSIS

Primarily intepreting the trends and what good looks like for features from thought leadership to detailed process maps.

JOURNEY REDESIGN

Using the needs and expectations of our two user definitions, I crafted a first recommendation journey based on both customer insight and what good looks like in paralell markets.

UI HANDOVER

I handed over my loose sketches to UI designers, making sure to communicate the user needs, most important features to elevate and best in class feature stimuli to guide their design process.

What I learned on this project

Being primarily a brand strategy project, this really taught me how applicable the customer focused experience frameworks I work with everyday are to de-risking projects. Whether its porduct, brand or process, the same methodologies and practises of thorough validation can result in a solid outcome based on truth.
