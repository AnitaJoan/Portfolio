Recent work

In April 2019, I completed a 10 week UX Design Immersive course at General Assembly London. Here I developed 3 concept projects and 1 client project. I also designed and coded this website!

Redesigning The Beauty & the Taste Wine Website

CLIENT - 2.5 WEEK SPRINT - 4 PERSON TEAM

Brief

Reduce bounce rate and improve conversion

Solution

Our biggest challenge was to balance business goals with user goals. User testing was vital at every stage. We redesigned problem pages to focus more on sales and removed the mysticism from the information architecture and content hierarchy.

Gamification of the British Museum Experience

CONCEPT - 2 WEEK SPRINT - 4 PERSON TEAM

Brief

Enhance museum visitor engagement with exhibits

Solution

We consolidated the data gained from contextual inquiry, user interviews and our design studio ideation and we developed a game that connected museum artefacts with a storyline. This turned the museum experience into a scavenger hunt.

Plenti Household Stock Manager App

CONCEPT - 2 WEEK SPRINT - SOLO

Brief

To solve my partners problem which was forgetting to buy groceries we often run out of until its too late

Solution

This first project centered around the research and definition phase of UX. I learned how to gain insights from interviews and user testing and translate these into actionable solutions. I developed an app to calculate the use of household essentials and set reminders to buy them.

Sunshine Foods Grocery Website

CONCEPT - 2 WEEK SPRINT - SOLO

Brief

Create an online website that establishes trust in fresh products and rewards loyalty while maintaining the local store feel.

Solution

I studied the Information Architecture and card sorting data to create an intuitive navigation and explored UX best practises to create a frictionless account creation and checkout flow.
