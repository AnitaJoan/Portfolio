My Process

I use the Double Diamond framework to tackle UX problems.
My process is not always linear but adaptable to the specific brief and timeframe. You never know when you might need to revisit research, conduct more tests or double check on the competition!

CONCEPT - 2 WEEK SPRINT - 4 PERSON TEAM

Brief
