Design Process

Design processes are a part of my toolbox just as maps, methods and software are. My process is constantly evolving through practise, research and chatting with people in tech. I think knowing which tools to use and when is key to being a good UX designer.

The ‘why’ behind design

After having been a Lead Graphic Designer for 5 years, I moved into UX/UI design to find the ‘why’ behind my designs. It frustrates me that poor content and confusing UX stops people from getting on to achieve their every goal in life. I want to design solutions to help people grow, move forward or simply get life admin done.
